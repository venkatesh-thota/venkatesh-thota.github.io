Heroes of World Cup 2011

Yuvraj Singh

Yuvraj singh was the man of the series of world cup 2011.

He was the Highest run Scorrer of tournament.

He played a key role in india winning the Icc World Cup 2011.

He Excelled with his all round perfoemance in all departments of the game.

Sachin Tendulkar

sachin was the second highest run scorrer of ICC World Cup 2011.

He Scored two Centuries and two half Centuries

sachin played a crucial part in India winning world Cup in 2011

M S Dhoni

M.S Dhoni was the man of match in ICC World Cup 2011 Final Match

Dhoni played a Match Winning Knock in the final Match

Dhoni's Brilliant Captaincy led India to win in world Cup Final

Tillakaratne Dilshan

Dilshan was the highest Run scorer of the series.

Dilshan scored two centuries and two half centuries.

Though he was not a part of winning team but played a crucial role in making his team reach finals.

He has a Highest Score of 131 against zimbabwe in Icc world cup 2011.

Shahid Afridi

shahid Afridi was the highest wicket taker of Icc world cup 2011.

He has taken a total of 21 wickets.

Afridi also excelled with bat he has scored 84 runs with a strike rate of 125.

He played crucial role in making his team reach semi-finals.

Zaheer Khan

Zaheer Khan was highest wicket taker of ICC Cricket world Cup.

Zaheer has taken a total of 21 wickets.

Zaheer khan played a important role in india winning ICC Cricket World Cup in 2011.
